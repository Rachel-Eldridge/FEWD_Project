About Teachers Learning

Character forged through learning

Some might call us a micro-school. We call ourselves 30 Acres: a one-room schoolhouse nestled in the farmlands of Elizabeth, Indiana.

Bible, good literature, Latin, mathematics, learning facts (Bible, history, etc.) in fun ways through music, art, story and drama, science with hands-on activities, penmanship -- yes, even cursive will be introduced and taught! Classical approach with a Charlotte Mason twist. Balance.

Meet the teachers

Mrs. Sheila has a BA in art education at the University of Cincinnati; several years of Christian classroom teaching (English, math, science, German, art, music, yearbook, physical education, and health!), home schooled children and taught art and music lessons for home schooled children. Last year she did long term substitution for 8th, 9th and 10th grade English teaching Shakespeare. Currently, she teaches at KaySera Farm home school art lessons at the farm, teaches flute at Lanesville, private piano and flute lessons as well as teaching preschool aged children and adult art lessons as well.

Mr. Doug is a graduate of Taylor University in Political Science, Philosophy, and an associates in Business Administration. He has worked in the financial world for over 25 years. Has taught adult Sunday School classes. Currently Mr. Doug writes weekly Bible studies and delivers them at the local library. He is a polymath.
